Priority Operator Description Examples
1 | or  OR
 Logical "or" (Vertical bar). Filter the column for content that matches text from either side of the bar.
 <20 | >40 (matches a column cell with either "<20" or ">40")
2 && or AND
 Logical "and". Filter the column for content that matches text from either side of the operator.
 >20 && >40 (matches a column cell that contains both ">20" and "<40")
3 /\d/
 Add any regex to the query to use in the query ("mig" flags can be included /\w/mig)
 /b[aeiou]g/i (finds "bag", "beg", "BIG", "Bug", etc);>/r$/ (matches text that ends with an "r")
4 < <= >= >
 Find alphabetical or numerical values less than or greater than or equal to the filtered query .
 <=10 (find values greater than or equal to 10)
5 ! or !=
 Not operator, or not exactly match. Filter the column with content that do not match the query. Include an equal (=), single (') or double quote (") to exactly not match a filter.


 !e (find text that doesn't contain an "e");!"ELISA" (find content that does not exactly match "ELISA")
6 " or =
 To exactly match the search query, add a quote, apostrophe or equal sign to the beginning and/or end of the query
 abc" or abc= (exactly match "abc")
7 - or to
 Find a range of values. Make sure there is a space before and after the dash (or the word "to").
 10 - 30 or 10 to 30 (match values between 10 and 30)
8 ?
 Wildcard for a single, non-space character.
 S?c (finds "Sec" and "Soc", but not "Seec")
8 *
 Wildcard for zero or more non-space characters.
 B*k (matches "Black" and "Book")
9 ~
 Perform a fuzzy search (matches sequential characters) by adding a tilde to the beginning of the query

About US

BEAM by Fincrese offers financial services for students to help them build a solid foundation for financial exploration.
BEAM is forged with the purpose of illuminating the path of financial freedom for the youth of India. Fincrese is fascinated by the rupee coin. It is small, but almighty. It is abundant, yet priceless. It is light but also weighs down the pocket of a fellow who is financially insecure. BEAM is here to lighten the burden of the coin and transform it into happiness, warmth, fulfillment, and contentment in the long run.

We at BEAM believe in and wholeheartedly embody the “One for All” soulset. We believe that the power to transform one’s financial future lies in their hands; BEAM is here to help you harness and provide a stable financial future. We envision a world where the youth are freed from the shackles of financial insecurity.
We respect and are mindful about the various inhibitions that a student might face. Keeping those in mind, we designed our services that cater to the specific needs of a student to help them reach a higher indifference curve.
